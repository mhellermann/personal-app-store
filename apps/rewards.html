Non-flight redemption

Gift cards, statement credit, cash, hotel transfers, portal bookings. Value per point is simply value received ÷ points. The bar here is the ~1 cpp cash floor — anything below it is strictly worse than just taking the cash.

Rule of thumb: cash, statement credit, gift cards and the travel portal all cluster near 1 cpp. Hotel transfers usually net ~0.5–1 cpp. None of these is a "win" — they're the floor your flight redemptions need to beat.
